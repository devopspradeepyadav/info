Google Free Online Training Course | #CloudOnBoard

Explore new data-driven possibilities during #GoogleCloudOnBoard: Unleash Your Data Potential on October 27th and 28th. Discover solutions and strategies that can give you faster, smarter insights–without having to manage your own infrastructure. Take part in expert-led sessions and free online training and demos, and you’ll receive a special training discount for attending.

With the right data skills, the sky’s the limit. Expand yours at Cloud OnBoard: Unleash Your Data Potential, a free instructor-led training event. Explore the fundamentals of data analysis, machine learning, and data processing, then learn how to harness the full power of BigQuery. Join us for one day or both and take your knowledge to the next level.

October 27 | Big Data and Machine Learning 

October 28 | Data Warehousing with BigQuery
