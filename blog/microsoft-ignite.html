Microsoft Ignite Cloud Skills Challenge

Home Blog MICROSOFT CLOUD SKILLS CHALLENGE

Earn a free Microsoft Certification exam and chances to win more prizes

Complete challenges, earn sweepstakes entries There are six individual challenges within the larger Microsoft Ignite Cloud Skills Challenge contest. Each challenge is a curated collection of Microsoft Learn modules. Register for and complete as many of the challenges as you'd like. Remember that the more challenges you complete, the more entries you'll earn towards the sweepstakes prize.

Complete your first challenge, earn a free Microsoft Certification exam


After you've completed your first challenge, you'll earn a free Microsoft Certification exam. You will get an email around October 15th with more details on how to redeem your free Microsoft Certification.

In order to receive your free Microsoft Certification exam notification email, you must have an email address for notifications in your Learn profile. You can add one by signing into your Learn profile settings, updating the Manage email preferences section and hitting save.
After you complete your collection, the leaderboard may take up to an hour to reflect your module completion. Please wait an hour or two before emailing us to check in on your status.


Complete the modules before the challenge closes on October 7, 2020 at 7:00AM UTC.

The Microsoft Ignite Cloud Skills Challenge
