Edx: The Macquarie Group Scholarship Program Answers

It means you will get a 100% Discount on the certificate. You can change these answers according to your needs. Don’t copy the Exact Answer (until you are lazy, like me).

If you are creative enough then main points to write the Answer for this question:

Why are you applying for this scholarship?

  • Tell them about your Financial background.

  • Your current educational scenario and future reference.
  • Your interest in the topic.

What impact will this scholarship have on your academic or professional plans?

Google Cloudstitch is very comfortable for the Android Operating System. Data Science is my dream. I also started learning Python programming. Edx is providing Data Science with Python and I want to grab this opportunity. Having a Certificate is proof for listing in my resume. I will have knowledge as well as a Certificate. This certificate helps me for grabbing the job in the future. After this course, I will do another course related to Data Science and Python Programming. I also did an Artificial Intelligence Basic course for understanding machine learning. I want to learn machine learning deeply. It will help me a lot in the future. Thank you very much again for these best courses.

Answer 2 :

I am pursuing my graduation in Computer Science. Also, I have a keen interest in learning new programming languages and I feel like embedded systems will be emerging rapidly in the technology industry in the coming decade and I have a keen interest in learning the facts that how do these microcontrollers work, which controls the very heart of the embedded system. I want to pursue my career in the internet of things and the microcontroller industry. I want to complete the course due to my curiosity and also that I can put a good resume to get applied to a job. This course will boost my job prospects after graduation from my institute.
