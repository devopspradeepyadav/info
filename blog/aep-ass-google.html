Android Enterprise Platform Associate Assessment Answers

This course can be accessed by clicking here

Solution for the Post Assessment

  1. Device Admin API’s should be used for deployments when: Device Admin API’s should not be used going forward as they are being deprecated
  2. On personally owned Android devices featuring Android (5.0+) what feature does a “Work Profile” provide? Create a company managed work profile to keep all your work and personal data
  3. What feature of the Android platform protects 99.99% of devices from potentially harmful applications? Google Play Protect
  4. ___________ enrollment enables large scale over the air bulk Android (8.0+) deployments across multiple device makers so organizations can mobilize their employees with ease and no manual setup. Zero-touch
  5. What is Android Enterprise? A modern management framework with robust enterprise APIs and secure app deployment via managed Google Play.
  6. Which of the following statements is true pertaining to company-owned, fully managed device enrollment: To enroll a device in a fully managed state, devices must be enrolled from a fresh out of the box state.
  7. Which Google led and validated program makes it simple for businesses to confidently select, deploy and manage Android devices and services that meet elevated enterprise requirements? Android Enterprise Recommended
  8. What are the four layers of security that keep Android devices safe? A multi-layered approach from hardware, OS, management APIs and Google services such as Google Play Protect
  9. Which of the following statements is true as it pertains to device management with Android Enterprise? Android Enterprise offers a management option for any ownership or deployment model: from work profile, strict lock‑down devices to single‑use scenarios.
  10. What are the key selling points regarding Android Enterprise? Comprehensive and innovative mobile OS platform providing extensive management API’s, multi-layer device and app security, and a global infrastructure for secure app distribution.
  11. What percentage of Android phones are free of potentially harmful apps? 99.95%
  12. What feature of Android intercepts malware threats in real time? Google Play Protect
  13. With an Android Enterprise Work Profile you can: Create a company managed “Work Only” profile to keep all personal data separate.
  14. The feature that gives IT control over company data while allowing workers to keep their pictures and apps private is called: Work profile
  15. What are the key selling points regarding Android for enterprise? Comprehensive and innovative mobile OS platform providing extensive management API’s, multi-layer device and app security, and a global infrastructure for secure app distribution.
  16. Which deployment option allows for bulk company owned devices to be deployed without individual set-up? Zero-touch
  17. Which of the following is true for work applications in the Android for Work profile? They are marked by a distinct orange briefcase icon.
  18. A managed version of the Google Play store for your business allows an organization to: (i) Monitor all device activity (ii) Control all device activity
  19. What percentage of Android phones using Google Play are free of potentially harmful apps? 99.95%
  20. The Managed Google Play store allows IT admins to: Whitelist apps for easy deployment, allow silent app installs and private app hosting
  21. What Android feature are customers most attracted to? (Will Not Count Towards Score) (i) Shareability of devices between employees. (ii) Speed of boot up time and system performance.
